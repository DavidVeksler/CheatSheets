Advanced autonomous systems and defense technology.

Lattice Platform

Lattice OS

AI-powered open operating system for defense, enabling autonomous sensemaking, command & control, and connecting hardware.

Key Capabilities:
  • Command & Control: Real-time battlespace understanding, decision support, and action orchestration.
  • Mission Autonomy: Enables control of diverse autonomous assets for complex missions.
  • Sensor Fusion: Integrates data from various sensors into a common operating picture.
  • AI/ML Driven: Leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning for detection, classification, and tracking.
Lattice Mesh‚Ñ¢

Decentralized mesh networking for secure data distribution across domains, platforms, and distances, even in DIL environments.

Key Features:
  • Resilient Comms: Operates in degraded, disconnected, intermittent, low-bandwidth (DDIL) conditions.
  • Decentralized: No single point of failure, increasing robustness.
  • Secure Transport: Ensures data integrity and confidentiality.
  • Scalable: Connects numerous Anduril and third-party systems.
Lattice SDK‚Ñ¢

Software Development Kit enabling partners to build and integrate applications and hardware with the Lattice Platform.

Key Benefits for Partners:
  • Developer Resources: Access to sandboxes, documentation, and sample applications.
  • Seamless Integration: Tools for integrating third-party capabilities into Lattice.
  • Tactical Edge Deployment: Facilitates creation of solutions for austere environments.
  • Ecosystem Growth: Part of the Lattice Partner Program.

Force Protection

Counter UAS

Detects, tracks, identifies, and intercepts unmanned aircraft and autonomous drone systems using a layered, Lattice-powered approach.

Key Components & Capabilities:
  • Detection & Tracking: Sentry Towers (Long Range), Wisp, Pulsar.
  • Identification: AI-driven classification via Lattice.
  • Interception: Anvil/Anvil-M (kinetic), Roadrunner-M (interceptor), Pulsar (EW).
  • End-to-End Kill Chain: Managed through Lattice OS.
Counter Intrusion (Land)

Automates protection of bases and critical infrastructure by autonomously identifying and surfacing land-based threats.

Key Components & Capabilities:
  • Persistent Surveillance: Sentry Towers (various models), Ghost sUAS.
  • Wide-Area Sensing: Wisp for passive IR detection.
  • AI-Powered Analysis: Lattice OS for threat detection and classification.
  • Scalable Defense: Protects perimeters of any size.
Maritime Counter Intrusion

Provides autonomous, persistent security for shorelines, ports, and maritime assets against surface and subsurface threats.

Key Components & Capabilities:
  • Surface Detection: Maritime Sentry Towers, Wisp.
  • Underwater Surveillance: Dive-LD, Dive-XL, Seabed Sentry.
  • Aerial Support: Ghost sUAS.
  • Integrated C2: Lattice OS for a comprehensive maritime picture.

Air Systems

Altius

Versatile, multi-domain launched (air, land, sea) autonomous loitering munition for ISR&T, kinetic strikes, and EW.

Key Features:
  • Variants: Altius-600/600M, Altius-700/700M (heavier payload).
  • Roles: ISR&T, kinetic engagement, RF decoy, SIGINT, comms relay, cyber warfare.
  • Autonomy: Collaborative teaming and coordinated strikes via Lattice.
  • Endurance: Best-in-class loitering time and range.
Anvil / Anvil-M

Autonomous kinetic interceptor for precise, low-collateral defeat of Group 1 & 2 UAS threats, cued by Lattice OS.

Key Features:
  • Variant: Anvil-M (munition with high-explosive payload).
  • Guidance: Autonomous navigation and terminal guidance.
  • Deployment: Anvil Launch Box for ruggedized, transportable deployment.
  • Integration: Key part of Anduril's cUAS solution.
Barracuda / Barracuda-M

Family of air-breathing Autonomous Air Vehicles (AAVs) for hyper-scale production; munition variant for cruise missile capability.

Key Features:
  • Variants: Barracuda-100, Barracuda-250, Barracuda-500.
  • Design: Simplified for mass employment and agile hyper-scale production.
  • Capability: Barracuda-M offers affordable, producible, adaptable cruise missile alternative.
  • Software-Defined: Upgradable with novel autonomous behaviors.
Bolt / Bolt-M

Man-packable, modular Autonomous Air Vehicle (AAV) for rapid response situational awareness and precision firepower.

Key Features:
  • Variant: Bolt-M (munition variant).
  • Roles: ISR, Search & Rescue, Precision Strike (Bolt-M).
  • Deployment: Rapidly deployable by a single operator.
  • Autonomy: Onboard AI for automated tracking and navigation.
Fury

High-performance, multi-mission Group 5 autonomous air vehicle (AAV) enabling collaborative autonomy for the high-end fight.

Key Features:
  • Performance: High-speed (.95M), high-G (9Gs) capabilities.
  • Modularity: Rapid reconfiguration for various payloads (RF, IR).
  • Autonomy: Leverages Lattice for Mission Autonomy for manned-unmanned teaming.
  • Design: Model-driven, field-tested, designed for accelerated fielding and affordable mass.
Ghost / Ghost-X

Expeditionary, quiet, modular VTOL sUAS for ISR, targeting, and force protection with intuitive autonomy. (Blue UAS Cleared).

Key Features:
  • Variant: Ghost-X (expanded range, endurance, payload).
  • Deployment: Man-portable, assembled in <2 mins.
  • Autonomy: Onboard computer vision for detection, classification, tracking. Intelligent teaming via Lattice.
  • Payloads: Modular bays for multi-mission flexibility.
Iris

Family of airborne autonomous imaging & targeting sensors for IRST, missile warning, visualization, and targeting applications.

Key Features:
  • Technology: Proprietary Computational Pixel Imager (CPI) for real-time AI processing at the edge.
  • Applications: Infrared Search and Track (IRST), missile warning, targeting.
  • Configurable: Various lens types, wavelengths, and pixel formats.
  • Design: Low SWaP, modular, designed for affordability and scalable manufacturing.
Pulsar

Family of software-defined Electromagnetic Warfare (EW) systems leveraging AI at the edge to rapidly adapt to emerging threats.

Key Features:
  • Variants: Pulsar (fixed-site), Pulsar-L (compact), Pulsar-A (airborne), Pulsar-V (vehicle).
  • Adaptability: AI-enabled for rapid counter to new threats.
  • Capabilities: ECM, CUxS, Direction Finding, Geolocation.
  • Networked EW: Multiple systems for coordinated effects and geolocation.
Roadrunner / Roadrunner-M

Reusable VTOL AAV with twin turbojets and modular payloads; Roadrunner-M is a high-explosive interceptor for air defense.

Key Features:
  • Reusability: VTOL for launch, loiter, and return/reuse at near-zero cost.
  • Performance: High subsonic speed, high-G maneuverability.
  • Autonomy: Operator-supervised, enabling one operator for many Roadrunners.
  • Associated: Nest (networked, automated hangar for transport, maintenance, launch).

Underwater Systems

Copperhead / Copperhead-M

Family of high-speed Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) for intelligent on-demand capabilities; munition variant for torpedo-like effects.

Key Features:
  • Variants: Copperhead-100/100M, Copperhead-500/500M.
  • Speed: >30 kts with advanced electric propulsion.
  • Deployment: Mountable hull for launch from autonomous host vehicles (e.g., Dive-XL).
  • Autonomy: AI-enabled edge compute for object detection, tracking, obstacle avoidance.
Dive-LD

Reliable and flexible Large Displacement AUV for littoral and deep-water (up to 6000m) survey, inspection, and ISR.

Key Features:
  • Depth: Up to 6000 meters.
  • Endurance: Up to 10 days, scalable for multi-week missions.
  • Payloads: Flexible architecture for rapid integration of complex/multi-sensor suites.
  • Manufacturing: Unique 3D printed exterior for rapid customization.
Dive-XL

Extra Large AUV (XL-AUV) with highly configurable architecture for multiple large payloads and long-duration missions.

Key Features:
  • Payload Capacity: Can carry up to three payloads or one extra large payload.
  • Deployment: Fits in standard 40' shipping container; can deploy Seabed Sentries.
  • Operations: All-electric powertrain for extended undersea operations without surfacing.
  • Applications: Seabed survey, ISR, strike missions.
Seabed Sentry

AI-enabled sensor forming a wireless underwater network for real-time sensing, communication, and persistent monitoring.

Key Features:
  • Networked Sensing: Forms a Lattice-connected network when deployed at scale.
  • Mission Lifetime: Months to years, depth rating >500M.
  • Comms: LF/VLF Acoustic Communications (ACOMMS) Relay.
  • Deployment: Can be autonomously deployed by Dive-XL.

Ground Systems & Sensors

Menace

Family of configurable, expeditionary C4 solutions for operators at the edge, extending Lattice Mesh capabilities.

Key Features & Variants:
  • Purpose: Turnkey C4 for austere, disconnected environments.
  • Menace-I: Classified C4 in SCIF/SAPF accreditable shelter.
  • Menace-X: On-the-move C4 solution.
  • Menace-T: Man-portable, compact C4 system.
  • Integration: Supports Anduril and third-party software/hardware.
Sentry Towers

Autonomous awareness towers using AI for detection, identification, and tracking of objects across land, sea, and air.

Key Features & Variants:
  • AI-Enabled: Onboard edge processing for real-time analysis.
  • Variants: Standard Range, Long Range (cUAS), Maritime, Extended Range, Mobile Sentry, Cold Weather.
  • Power: Solar-powered options available, infrastructure independent.
  • Deployment: Rapidly deployable, can be online in hours.
Wisp

Wide-Area Infrared System for Persistent Surveillance (WISP), providing passive, 360-degree, AI-enabled imaging for threat detection.

Key Features:
  • Passive Detection: Cannot be jammed, suitable for contested environments.
  • 360¬∞ Coverage: Continuous, wide-area surveillance day and night.
  • AI-Enabled: Automated threat detection and situational awareness.
  • Multi-Domain: Adaptable for land, sea, and air applications (base security, cUAS, maritime).

Rocket Motors

Solid Rocket Motors (SRMs)

Full-service, high-volume supplier of conventional and next-generation solid rocket motors for defense and space.

Key Capabilities:
  • Custom Design & Production: Bespoke SRMs for diverse applications.
  • High-Volume Manufacturing: Facilities for 3,000+ SRMs per year.
  • Energetics Qualified: Compliant with DoD standards for Hazard Class Materials.
  • On-Site Testing: Comprehensive test and inspection tools.
ALITEC

Proprietary aluminum-lithium alloy fuel enhancing solid rocket motor performance for extended range and speed.

Key Benefits:
  • Performance Boost: Increases range, speed to target.
  • Payload Capacity: Allows for expanded payload capacity compared to conventional aluminum.
  • Integration: Can be integrated into various propellant formulations.
  • Advanced Material: Developed and manufactured by Anduril.
Denali

High-performance 18-inch solid rocket booster designed to advance hypersonic capabilities affordably.

Key Features:
  • Application: Hypersonic systems.
  • Manufacturing: Utilizes improved single-piece-flow and proprietary tech like bladeless speedmixing.
  • Cost-Effective: Designed for large volume production at reduced cost.
  • Performance: High-thrust capabilities for boosting hypersonic vehicles.
