Operational Security (OPSEC)

The First Rule of Bitcoin Club CRITICAL

Nobody should know you own Bitcoin. Period. Most attacks start with information leakage.

Never reveal holdings: Not even "I own some crypto" at parties
Implementation:
  • Create separate "crypto" vs "normie" social media accounts
  • Use pseudonyms for all crypto-related activities
  • Never post wallet screenshots or transaction IDs
  • Decline all podcast/media appearances about your "crypto journey"
  • Tell family you "lost it all in the crash" if pressed
Real Attack Vector:

Jameson Lopp documented 100+ physical Bitcoin attacks. 95% started with public knowledge of holdings.

Address Privacy HIGH

Your home address linked to Bitcoin ownership = targeted robbery risk.

Protection Layers:
  • Use LLC + registered agent for property ownership
  • P.O. Box or mail forwarding for all crypto-related mail
  • Never use home address on exchange KYC (use office/PO Box)
  • Remove address from public records (use services like DeleteMe)
  • Different billing/shipping addresses for hardware wallets
Pro Tip:

Establish residency in privacy-friendly states (Wyoming, Delaware) for LLC formation.

Physical Security

Home Fortress Protocol HIGH

Layered physical security deters and delays $5 wrench attacks.

Minimum Setup ($5K-15K):
  • Perimeter: 8ft fence, motion lights, "Beware of Dog" signs
  • Cameras: 4K PoE system with 30-day NVR + cloud backup
  • Doors: Grade 1 deadbolts, reinforced frames, security film on windows
  • Alarm: Professional monitoring with cellular backup
  • Safe Room: Reinforced master bedroom, separate alarm zone
Advanced ($15K+):
  • Ballistic panels in walls
  • Panic buttons in every room
  • License plate recognition cameras
  • Security consultant assessment
Reality: Goal is 5-10 minute delay for police response, not Fort Knox
Personal Defense MEDIUM

When security layers fail, compliance + misdirection saves lives.

Under Duress Protocol:
  1. Comply immediately - Your life > Bitcoin
  2. Decoy wallet ready - Hardware wallet with ~$10K
  3. Duress passphrase - Reveals decoy, not main funds
  4. Time-locked setup - "I can't access for 48 hours"
  5. Geographic distribution - "Other keys are in safety deposit box"
Preparation:
  • Krav Maga / tactical training (situational awareness > fighting)
  • Concealed carry (if legal and trained)
  • Medical training (Stop the Bleed course)
  • Practice duress scenarios with family

Technical Security Architecture

Multi-Signature Setup CRITICAL

Single points of failure = single points of catastrophic loss.

Recommended: 2-of-3 Multisig
  • Key 1: Hardware wallet at home (quick access)
  • Key 2: Hardware wallet in bank safety deposit box
  • Key 3: Offline backup with attorney/trust company
Setup Requirements:
  • Use different hardware wallet manufacturers (Coldcard + Ledger + Trezor)
  • Generate keys on air-gapped devices
  • Test recovery process quarterly
  • Document process for inheritors

Inheritance & Succession

Dead Man's Switch Protocol CRITICAL

Your Bitcoin dies with you unless you plan. Technical + legal + practical = successful transfer.

Three-Layer Inheritance System:
  1. Legal Layer:
    • Revocable trust holding LLC that "owns" Bitcoin
    • Clear successor trustees named
    • Instructions for digital asset handling
  2. Technical Layer:
    • Sealed envelope with attorney: wallet type, derivation paths
    • Time-locked transaction as backup (nLockTime)
    • Unchained Capital or Casa inheritance service
  3. Practical Layer:
    • Annual "Bitcoin fire drill" with spouse
    • Video walkthrough stored with lawyer
    • Trusted Bitcoin-literate executor
    • Clear instructions: "Call [Expert] at [Number] immediately"
Warning: "I'll teach them someday" = your heirs get nothing

Disaster Preparedness

Geographic Redundancy MEDIUM

Natural disasters are localized. Your Bitcoin must not be.

Distribution Strategy:
  • Minimum 100 miles between backup locations
  • Different disaster profiles (not all in earthquake zones)
  • International safety deposit box (Switzerland/Singapore)
  • Cloud-encrypted backup (as last resort only)
Access During Crisis:
  • Memorize 1 location's access method
  • Emergency fund in multiple jurisdictions
  • Satellite phone for communication
  • Bug-out bag with hardware wallet
Memory Wallet LOW

Last resort: Your mind as the ultimate cold storage.

Implementation:
  • Memorize 12-word seed (not 24 - too risky)
  • Use memory palace technique
  • Small amount only (~$50K emergency fund)
  • Practice weekly recitation
  • Never your primary storage method
Warning: Head trauma, age, stress = forgotten Bitcoin
