Rituals & Festivals

Life‑Cycle Milestones

Ritual markers for each stage of life:

  • Brit Milah / Simchat Bat – covenantal entry & naming.
  • Bar/Bat Mitzvah – assumption of mitzvah responsibility.
  • Chuppah & Ketubah – sanctified marriage contract.
  • Shiva & Yahrzeit – structured mourning & memory.
Festival Cycle

Annual rhythm of sacred time:

  • Rosh Hashanah – New Year, shofar, introspection.
  • Yom Kippur – Day of Atonement, fasting, repentance.
  • Passover – Exodus seder, matzah, freedom.
  • Shavuot – Torah revelation, all‑night study, dairy.
  • Sukkot – Harvest booths, lulav & etrog, hospitality.
  • Hanukkah – Oil miracle, menorah lighting 8 nights.
  • Purim – Esther story, costumes, charity, feasting.
