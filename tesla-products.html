Internal Technical Specification Framework

Blueprint for detailed documentation on all Tesla products.

I. Electric Vehicles (EVs)

General Aspects for ALL EVs

Core technical specifications common to all Tesla electric vehicle platforms.

1. Overall Vehicle Architecture:
  • Design Philosophy & Core Principles
  • Platform Strategy
  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
  • Vehicle Generations/Revisions
  • Regulatory Compliance & Homologation
2. Powertrain System:
  • Battery Pack System: Cell Technology (Chemistry, Form Factor, Specs), Module Design, Pack Assembly (Configuration, Voltage, Capacity, Structural Integration, HV Interconnects, Enclosure, Serviceability), BMS (Architecture, Algorithms, Safety), Thermal Management System (TMS).
  • Drive Unit(s): Electric Motor(s) (Type, Configuration, Power, Torque, Cooling), Power Inverter(s) (Technology, Control, Cooling), Gearbox/Transmission.
  • On-Board Charger (OBC)
  • DC-DC Converter (HV to LV)
3. Chassis and Body:
  • Body Structure: Materials, Joining Technologies, Crash Structures, Aerodynamics.
  • Suspension System: Type, Components, Active Control (if applicable).
  • Steering System: Type, EPS, Steer-by-Wire (if applicable).
  • Braking System: Regenerative, Friction, Actuation.
  • Wheels and Tires.
4. Low-Voltage (LV) Electrical System
  • LV Battery
  • Power Distribution Architecture
  • Body Control Module (BCM) / Vehicle Control Unit (VCU)
5. Software, Electronics, and Autonomy
  • Central Compute Architecture
  • Operating System(s) & Software Stack
  • Autopilot / Full Self-Driving (FSD) System: Sensor Suite (Cameras, Radar, Ultrasonics, LiDAR - if present, GNSS/IMU), Perception Subsystem, Planning & Control Subsystem, Hardware Revisions.
  • Infotainment System (IVI)
  • Telematics Control Unit (TCU)
  • Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) & Software Over-The-Air (SOTA) Updates
  • Vehicle Network Architecture
6. Interior Systems
  • Seats, HVAC System, Interior Lighting, Dashboard & Controls, Airbag & Restraint Systems
7. Exterior Systems
  • Exterior Lighting, Wipers & Washers, Door Systems, Glass
8. Safety & Security
  • Active & Passive Safety Systems, High-Voltage Safety, Cybersecurity, Anti-theft System
9. Manufacturing & Assembly
  • Key Manufacturing Processes, Production Line Layout, Quality Control
10. Serviceability & Diagnostics
  • Diagnostic Ports, Remote Diagnostics, Service Tools, Modularity
11. Key Differentiating Technologies
12. Integration with other Tesla Products/Services
13. Known Limitations, Design Trade-offs
14. Revision History / Version Control
